I have been meaning to list the best blogs I usually read via my self hosted [miniflux](https://miniflux.app/), so you can also explore and add it to your list if you find it useful.

Some special mentions:
* _[Karan Sharma](https://mrkaran.dev/)_ - I personally learnt and got into [nomad](https://github.com/hashicorp/nomad) because of Karan and also contributed once upstream. He's an avid open source enthusiast, experiments a lot and share it across in his blog.
* _[Jake Howard](https://theorangeone.net/posts/)_ - Most of my self-hosted infra is inspired by [Jake's infrastructure](https://git.theorangeone.net/systems/infrastructure). He writes a lot on [self hosting](https://theorangeone.net/posts/tags/self-hosting/).
* _[Sean Goedecke](https://www.seangoedecke.com/)_ - Writes more about politics in the corporate world. Sometimes developers need the harsh pills and should know about how a company thinks, behaves and rewards it's people.
* _[Lalit Maganti](https://lalitm.com/)_ - Similar to Sean, but also have some technical entries as well. I personally love his [essays](https://lalitm.com/page/essays/).
* _[Simon Willison](https://simonwillison.net/entries/)_ - Writes a lot on the recent developments in the field of LLMs.
